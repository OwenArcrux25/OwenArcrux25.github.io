Senren * Banka

  Hoori was a small town located in a remote, mountaineous region. Isolated from outside influence for decades, the town boasted an Edo era aesthetics like no other. Hoori was also famous for the annual sword-pulling event, which featured the legendary Murasamemaru embedded in a boulder (easiest to imagine Excalibur). Over the years, curious people from all over the world participated in the event and failed to pull the sword out, until Masaomi Arichi, originally dispatched by his parents to assist in an inn owned by his grandfather, accidentally broke the sword in half.
