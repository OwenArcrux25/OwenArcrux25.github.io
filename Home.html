Senren * Banka

  Hoori was a small town located in a remote, mountaineous region. Isolated from outside influence for decades, the town felt like frozen in the olden days, but it was precisely this trait that turned Hoori into a wonderful hot spring town. One of the most famous attractions was the legendary Murasamemaru katana embedded in a stone. Over the years, curious people tried and failed to pull the sword out; until Masaomi Arichi, originally dispatched by his parents to assist in an inn owned by his grandfather, accidentally broke the sword in half. To compensate, he had to marry into the Tomotake House which ran the shrine that housed Murasamemaru. Through his grandfather and the head shrine priest interference, Masaomi was betrothed to the princess shrine maiden, Yoshino Tomotake.

Cafe Stella & The Reaper's Butterfly

  Kousei Takamine was dead. Kousei Takamine had been involved in a fateful accident which unfortunately ended his life. Feeling greatly unsatisfied while dying a virgin, he miraculously rewinded time to before the accident and managed to survive. Just as he thought he had been safe, he was visited by a grim reaper who reaped his soul and left him unconscious. When he came to, he found himself in a room with a certain beautiful black-haired girl.
